What Is Bullying?

Bullying is were one causes harm to another: Verbally, Mentally, and Physically.

Bullying can cause people to break down mentally, sometimes leading them to harm themselves physically. (Self-Harm)

How To Stop Bullying!

If someone is getting bullied, help them.
